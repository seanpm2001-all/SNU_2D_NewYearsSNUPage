SNU New Years page¶

Welcome to the SNU New Years page!

As part of the plan to have embedded SNU pages for all holidays, a new years page was made for all SNU New Years celebration. This page was created very late, in January 22nd 2020, 23 days after it should have been started (I wasn't considering it at the time)

This page celebrates both SNU New Year anniversaries, and decades of the world and what defines them. This page accounts for all SNU New Years.
